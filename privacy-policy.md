# Privacy Policy — ALTOS

**Last updated: March 22, 2026**

## Introduction

ALTOS ("the App") is a time tracking application developed by Kevin Pan. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers.

### Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on your device:
- Time tracking records (arrival/departure times)
- Activity tracking data (client names, durations)
- Absence records (vacations, sick days, etc.)
- App settings and preferences (work hours, language, region)
- First name and last name (optional, used only for PDF export)

### iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud synchronization, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account managed by Apple. We have no access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage: [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy)

### Apple Watch

If you use the Apple Watch companion app, data is shared between your iPhone and Apple Watch via a shared App Group container on your devices. No data is sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time premium purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's terms govern all transactions: [apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/)

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are processed locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
